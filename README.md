# 🌾 GRAAMVAANI

**Tagline:** _"ग्रामस्य स्वरः, भविष्यस्य आधारः"_

---

## 📌 Project Overview

In a nation where villages are the soul of our civilization, **GRAAMVAANI** is a digital information and communication platform designed specifically for rural India. It delivers **government scheme updates, real-time crop prices, weather alerts, community discussions, SMS notifications, and multilingual AI assistance** directly to villagers through their mobile phones.

GRAAMVAANI bridges the long-standing gap between villages and vital information. It ensures that:

- Farmers receive **timely market prices and weather intelligence**
- Citizens stay **updated with government notices**
- Communities get a **digital platform to raise their collective voice**

At its core, GRAAMVAANI empowers rural citizens with the **right information at the right time**, making opportunity, awareness, and participation accessible to every village — **regardless of geography or connectivity**.

## 🛑 Problem Statement

Rural communities often lack **timely, reliable, and accessible information** due to:

- Limited internet connectivity  
- Language barriers  
- Fragmented government communication systems  
- Dependence on manual or word-of-mouth updates  

**GRAAMVAANI** solves this by acting as a **digital notice board combined with AI-powered assistance**, accessible even in **offline and low-bandwidth conditions**.

---

## ✨ Key Features

1. **Smart Government Notice Management System**  
   - Central & local notice integration  
   - OCR-based digitization of notices  

2. **Real-Time SMS Alert & Notification Engine**

3. **Offline-Enabled Progressive Web Application (PWA)**  
   - Works without internet and syncs automatically  

4. **Location-Based Weather Intelligence & Alert System**

5. **Farmer Advisory & Live Crop Price Intelligence**

6. **Community Engagement & Digital Discussion Forum**

7. **Multilingual Interface + Conversational Chatbot**

8. **Automated Expert & Service Appointment Scheduling**

9. **Smart Reminders & Workflow Automation**

10. **Secure Authentication & Data Protection Framework**

11. **Low-Bandwidth Optimization System**

12. **Voice-Based AI Assistance**  
    - Designed for non-literate users

## 🧠 Technical Architecture

### 🎨 Frontend
- **React** – Fast and modular UI  
- **PWA Architecture** – Offline-first experience  

### ⚙️ Backend
- **Node.js + Express** – Scalable server framework  
- **MongoDB** – Flexible document-based storage  

### 🔐 Authentication
- **Clerk** – Secure identity management  

### 🔄 Automation
- **n8n**  
  - Notice triggers  
  - Booking automation  
  - Notification workflows  

### 🤖 Conversational & Voice AI
- **Botpress** – Intelligent chatbot logic  
- **Twilio** – SMS and messaging integration  
- **ElevenLabs** – Emotional AI voice synthesis  
- **Google Text-to-Speech** – Multilingual voice support
